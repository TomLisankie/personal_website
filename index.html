Hi, I'm Emily Xie. I'm a professional software engineer living in NYC. My primary interests are code, art, and pedagogy.

Currently, I'm an artist-in-residence at Pioneer Works in Brooklyn, where I'm making art with code. In my spare time, I teach programming through various conference workshops and via Girl Develop It, a non-profit certified by the Board of Education that empowers women with tech skills. You can also catch me snapping portrait photography and razing villages in Age of Empires II.

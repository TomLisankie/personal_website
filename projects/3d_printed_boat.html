3D-PRINTED MOTOR BOAT

A fully motorized and steerable 3D-printed boat, designed and built from scratch.

My entry for the 2016 Red Hook Regatta 3D Printed Boat Race. This was primarily an exercise in 3D modeling, fabrication, and hardware. Various phases of fabrication pictured to the left.


YEAR: 2016

MADE WITH: Rhino, 3D Printer, Laser Cutter, Arduino, Adafruit NeoPixels LED Strip, 4-Channel Receiver/Transmitter Pair, Speed Controller, Steering Servo, 7.4V 5000mAh Battery

TAGS: code, hardware, fabrication
